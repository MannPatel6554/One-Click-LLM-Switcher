How It Works

2-Minute Setup

1

Download ZIP

Download the extension source package and extract it to a folder on your system.

2

Developer Mode

Go to chrome://extensions in your browser and enable the "Developer mode" toggle.

3

Load Unpacked

Click on "Load unpacked" and select the extracted extension folder. Ready to go!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. The extension is 100% serverless. Conversation logs never leave your browser memory and are handled locally inside your extension storage.

What if auto-paste fails?

We copy the chat history to your clipboard automatically. If the site blocks automatic paste, simply click the text field and press Ctrl+V.

Which platforms are supported?

We support full scraping and automated injection for Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and Grok, plus scraping from ChatGPT.
